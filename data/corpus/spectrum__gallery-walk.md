# Gallery Walk

The Gallery Walk is built into many active learning modalities. It ranges from a formal
 poster presentation to the quick stick of post-its to a public wall. In Team-Based
 Learning (TBL), a Gallery Walk often follows the step of simultaneous display.

In all forms, the Gallery Walk involves participants walking around the room (or virtual
 space) to appreciate the products of other students/student teams. In many cases,
 it can be valuable to have observers vote on the best display. This voting can be
 done using an online Google Form and students can do this quickly on their phones.

In online synchronous or ascynchronous learning, students can go on a Gallery Walk
 using Padlet. We have also enjoyed doing formal poster presentations in Second Life,
 a virtual world!
