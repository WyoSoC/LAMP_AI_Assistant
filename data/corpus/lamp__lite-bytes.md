# LAMP Lite Bytes

Don't have time for a full lecture or presentation? Rachel Watson's Lamp Lite Bytes vodcasts are exploring active learning techniques one small byte at a time! Each video, usually running around the 10 minutes in length, examines different techniques and methods along [The Active Learning Spectrum](https://www.uwyo.edu/science-initiative/lamp/active-learning-spectrum/index.html).
