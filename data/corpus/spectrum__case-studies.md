# Case Studies

Across disciplines, authentic cases experienced by practitioners are documented and
 can be used for student learning. Here, we provide some of our most liked resources:

- [The National Center for Case Study Teaching in Science](https://sciencecases.lib.buffalo.edu/) offers a searchable database of case studies.
- Harvard Univeristy offers case studies through their [Global Health Delivery Project](https://www.globalhealthdelivery.org/case-collection). Users can sign up to receive new cases as they become available.
- Case studies through [LifeLines Online](https://www.globalhealthdelivery.org/case-collection) is made possile by a collaboration between Southeast Missouri State University and
 BioQUEST at Beloit College and funding by the NSF.
